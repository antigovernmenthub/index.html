Mandate & Jurisdiction

Describes what this node reviews and what it deliberately ignores. Neutral language, no calls to action.

AntiGovernment operates as a documentation node focused on the collection and arrangement of publicly observable interactions between individuals and institutions. Materials are reorganized into case-style records to examine how procedures, policies, and discretion impact ordinary people.

  • Scope: Redacted screenshots, transcripts, and timelines drawn from publicly accessible channels.
  • Exclusions: Home addresses, phone numbers, and other direct identifiers are not stored or published.
  • Intent: Observation and documentation, not legal advice or coordination of harassment.
